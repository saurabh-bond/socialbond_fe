Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 20th Oct 2023

1. Cancellation Policy

At Sociobond, we strive to provide our users with a seamless and satisfactory experience. We understand that there may be situations where you need to cancel a service or request a refund. Please read this Cancellation and Refund Policy carefully to understand the procedures and terms related to cancellations and refunds.

1.1 Cancellation of Subscription

1.1.1 If you wish to cancel your subscription on the Sociobond website, you may do so at any time by accessing your account settings and following the cancellation instructions provided there. Upon cancellation, you will not be billed for any future subscription periods.

1.1.2 Please note that we do not provide refunds for the unused portion of your subscription period. Cancellation will only prevent future charges.

1.2 Cancellation of Services

1.2.1 If you wish to cancel a specific service, please contact our customer support team through the contact information provided on our website.

1.2.2 The availability of refunds for service cancellations will depend on the terms and conditions specific to that service. Please refer to the service-specific cancellation policy for more information.

2. Refund Policy

2.1 Refund Eligibility

2.1.1 Refunds will be issued based on the following criteria:

a. Subscription Cancellation: Refunds are not provided for the unused portion of your subscription period after cancellation. You will continue to have access to the services until the end of your current billing cycle.

b. Service Cancellation: Refund eligibility for services will vary depending on the specific service and its terms and conditions. Please refer to the service-specific cancellation policy for detailed refund information.

2.2 Requesting a Refund

2.2.1 To request a refund for an eligible service, please follow these steps:

a. Contact our customer support team via the contact information provided on our website.

b. Provide all necessary information, including your account details, order number, and a detailed explanation of your request.

2.2.2 Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date. Requests submitted after this period may not be considered.

2.3 Refund Processing

2.3.1 Once your refund request is received, we will review it to determine eligibility. The processing time may vary depending on the nature of the request and the specific service.

2.3.2 If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed using the same payment method you used for the original purchase. Refunds typically take 5-10 business days to be credited back to your account.

2.3.3 If your refund request is denied, you will receive an explanation for the decision. You may appeal the decision within 15 days of receiving the denial.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Cancellation and Refund Policy, please contact our customer support team via the contact information provided on our website. We are here to assist you and address any inquiries or issues you may have.

Sociobond reserves the right to update or modify this policy at any time. Users are encouraged to check this page periodically for the latest information on our Cancellation and Refund Policy.

By using the Sociobond website and its services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Cancellation and Refund Policy.
